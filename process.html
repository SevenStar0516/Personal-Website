Process

Planning & Rational

I plan to make a very simple personal website. I think simplicity is not only a design style, but also a style that can reflect a person. By simplifying design and content presentation, websites can communicate information more intuitively and provide a more enjoyable user experience. In today's age of information overload, concise typography and color matching enable users to easily browse content and quickly get the information they need. Flashy and excessive content and typographic content can confuse or overwhelm users. So I plan to use black and white as the main color, and with clear font, make the page look simple and clean. The plan is as follows:

  • Home page: A concise presentation of my personal information.
  • Portfolio: Displays personal work, such as videos, image collections, hobbies, etc.
  • Contact information: Provide contact information (mobile phone, Instagram, WeChat) and build a place where visitors can put their contact information.
  • Resume: Show my resume in form, so that HR can browse my information quickly and conveniently in the future work.
  • Workflow page: Show my design process.

Inspirations

My inspiration comes from the Apple website, which gives me a very clean and tidy experience and look. Each page has a clear structure and navigation that makes it easy for users to find the information they need. At the same time, the Apple website also uses large images and bright colors to attract users' attention, and I will consider this method in my design.

WCAG2.0

Perceivable

My web design is very visual, using a lot of images and large fonts to enhance the attractiveness of the page. Images play a finishing touch in web pages, which can not only convey information intuitively, but also bring users pleasant visual enjoyment. At the same time, the use of large fonts also makes the text more prominent, easy to read, and can effectively guide users to focus on important content. My web pages are mostly black and white. By using these two colors, I hope to give the user a simple and clear visual experience, and I do not like to use too many colors, because it might cause confusion. The contrast of white characters on a black background makes the text more eye-catching and easier to read and enhances the overall high-level feeling of the page. In addition, in the layout of the page, I adopted more white space to enhance the overall aesthetic of the page. These gaps not only make the content more prominent, but also bring users a comfortable and pleasant reading experience, without seeing too much information at once.

Operable

My web design is not only easy to navigate, but also focuses on user experience and ease of operation. The navigation bar is set at the top and left of the page, so that users can easily find what they need. In addition to the need to input user personal information on the contact page, other interactive elements can be accessed and operated by mouse, eliminating the tedious keyboard input steps. In order to improve the efficiency and convenience of users when using the web, I carefully designed various interactive elements. For example, a quick link module has been added to the secondary navigation bar (the left side of the page) to make it easy for users to jump directly to each title; At the same time, there are back to top and bottom buttons, allowing users to go back to the top of the page with one click.

Understandable

The text content I use is easy to understand, and each content section has a title. This is designed so that the reader can quickly understand what each section is about. I do not use abbreviations in my text, because they may lead to misunderstanding of information or difficulty in understanding, especially for the reader. Using whole words and sentences conveys my meaning more clearly and helps to avoid ambiguity.
